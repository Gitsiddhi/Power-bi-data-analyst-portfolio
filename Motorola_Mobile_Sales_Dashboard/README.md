Motorola Mobile Sales Dashboard

Objective
To analyze Motorola mobile sales performance across different cities, models, and customer segments to identify revenue trends and top-performing products.

---

Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX (Basic Measures)

---

Key Metrics (KPIs)
- Total Sales
- Total Quantity
- Total Transactions
- Average

---

Analysis Performed
- Compared sales performance by mobile model
- Identified high-revenue cities
- Analyzed customer ratings
- Evaluated preferred payment methods
- Visualized monthly sales trends

---

Business Insights
- Identified best-selling Motorola models
- Found top contributing cities to total revenue
- Highlighted seasonal demand patterns
- Discovered most preferred payment methods
